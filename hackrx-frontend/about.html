About HealthInsure AI

Our Vision

HealthInsure AI is built to simplify and modernize how people understand and interact with health insurance. We believe everyone deserves fast, intelligent help navigating policy terms, making claims, and finding the right insurance plans.

Key Features

  • Ask a Question: Get answers to your health insurance questions using natural language.
  • Upload Documents: Instantly summarize complex policy PDFs with AI.
  • Claim Assistance: Determine eligibility before submitting medical claims.
  • Policy Recommendations: Receive personalized policy suggestions based on your health profile and needs.

Built for HackRx 6.0

This project was developed as part of the HackRx 6.0 hackathon challenge, focusing on improving digital insurance experiences using AI.
